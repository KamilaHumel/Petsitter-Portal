## General info
<details>
<summary>Click here to see general information about <b>Petsitter-Portal</b>!</summary>
<br><b>Find the right person to look after your pet! :cat2: </b><br><br>
Have you ever gone somewhere and had no one to leave your pets with? <br>
Were you worried about leaving your pet in the care of the right person? <br> <br>
If the answer is <b>YES</b>, then Portal is perfect for you!:heart_eyes:<br><br>

The portal will help you find carers in your neighborhood. Before leaving your pet, you can provide the necessary information to a person of your choice regarding the care of your pet, its habits or special diet. After picking up the pet, you can rate the pet sitter and leave a comment about the service.
Also, if you are a pet sitter you can fully manage your profile. You can change your preferences at any time and only approve services when it suits you.
</details>

## Setup

<b>Clone the repo:</b> <br>
```git clone https://github.com/KamilaHumel/Petsitter-Portal.git```<br>

<b>Create a virtual environment to install dependencies:</b><br>
```virtualenv2 --no-site-packages env```<br>
```source env/bin/activate```<br>

<b>Install the dependencies:</b><br>
```pip install -r requirements.txt```<br>

<b>After the dependency download is complete:</b><br>
```cd Petsitter```<br>
```python manage.py runserver```<br>

<b>Navigate to:</b><br>
```http://127.0.0.1:8002/```
